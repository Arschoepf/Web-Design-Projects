Chattanooga, Tennessee

My favorite vacation was going to Chattanooga, TN. My dad and I went down during the summer because he had to go into the office down there. I really enjoyed it because I got to do a lot of cool stuff and see some awesome places.


While we were there, we went to lookout mountain and watched the suneset. It also offered a great view of the surrounding area. From the top, we could see 3 other states: South Carolina, Georgia, and Alabama.
